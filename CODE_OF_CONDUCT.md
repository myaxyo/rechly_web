# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Treat other contributors with respect. Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or bad-faith engagement are not acceptable in issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other project spaces.

## Expected Behavior

- Assume good intent and discuss technical disagreements directly.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect different levels of experience.
- Avoid sharing private information without consent.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or insults
- Harassment or stalking
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of project discussion

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors who violate this code of conduct.
